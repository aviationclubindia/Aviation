"Aviation is proof that given the will, we have the capacity to achieve the impossible, so aviate, navigate and communicate" pondered one of our founding members as they stared at the contrails soaring high into the sky, truly representing how limitless dreams are. Dreaming- being the first step, was soon followed by the forming of a small group over whatsapp with fellow future Aviators amidst the pandemic in 2020, which soon prospered into a discord server currently acting as a melting point of aviation lovers all over the world, ready to fly high and take over their dreams!!
